About Me

Who am I?

Hi, I’m EJ, a Full Stack Developer who enjoys turning complex problems into clean, maintainable solutions. I’m open to remote, freelance, and full-time work. My stack includes Laravel, Django, Node.js, and Vue.js, allowing me to work comfortably across both frontend and backend. What drives me is the curiosity to understand how things work under the hood. I enjoy digging into code, learning new tools, and solving bugs. I believe in writing clean and simple code that’s easy for any developer to understand and maintain.

My Journey

My journey began during my B.Sc. in Computer Science, fueled by a strong interest in computers. I started with HTML and CSS, and the joy of building sparked my drive to learn more—JavaScript, Python, and full-stack frameworks like Laravel. I began creating Python content to reinforce my learning, which also helped me sharpen my content creation skills and later explored AI tools to accelerate both content creation and web development. While still refining these skills, I’m now focused on finding real-world opportunities where I can grow, contribute, and become a more competent developer.

My Approach

I begin every project by understanding the client’s goals in detail. I define the required features, choose the right tech stack, and plan a clear roadmap. Then I build a minimum viable product (MVP) to test the core functionality. My focus is on clean, reusable components and a simple, clean UI. I optimize performance after the core build. I stay updated by following tech trends on Instagram and Twitter/X, and I ensure team clarity through well-documented code and meaningful comments. Above all, I believe in continuous learning and using the best tools to build better solutions.
